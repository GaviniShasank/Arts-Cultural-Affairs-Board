29 Aug

Orientation Day - [2024]

Orientation Day at CMRCET is the official welcome event for new students, designed to help them transition smoothly into college life. It’s a day filled with excitement as students meet their peers, explore the campus, and get acquainted with the resources and opportunities available at CMRCET. Through a series of informative sessions, campus tours, and interactive activities, Orientation Day introduces students to the academic expectations, extracurricular activities, and support services that will shape their college experience. It's the perfect start to their journey at CMRCET, setting the tone for a successful and enriching time ahead.

03 Aug

Graduation Day - [2024]

Graduation Day at CMRCET is a momentous occasion that celebrates the achievements of our students as they complete their academic journey. It’s a day filled with pride, joy, and reflection, as graduates receive their degrees in the presence of family, friends, and faculty. The event includes inspiring speeches, the presentation of awards, and the conferral of degrees, marking the transition from student life to the professional world. At CMRCET, Graduation Day is not just a ceremony; it's a celebration of hard work, perseverance, and the beginning of new opportunities.
